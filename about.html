About Our Program

The Northwestern University Swimming and Diving program is committed to excellence in both academics and athletics. Our program has a rich history of developing student-athletes who succeed in the pool and in the classroom.

Program Information

Our comprehensive program focuses on several key areas that define our commitment to student-athlete development:

  • Program history: Decades of tradition in competitive swimming and diving
  • Coaching staff: Experienced professionals dedicated to athlete development
  • Training facilities: State-of-the-art aquatic center and fitness facilities
  • Academic achievement: Supporting student-athletes in their educational goals
  • Community involvement: Active participation in local and university community service
